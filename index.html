About the Event

JOIN US FOR COACH 001 RUN 2024!

The Coach 001 Run 2024 is Mombasa's premier fitness event, taking place on 19th October 2024 at the scenic Bamburi Forest Trails. Whether you're an experienced athlete or just looking for a fun day out with family, this event offers something for everyone.

Every participant will receive a custom-branded t-shirt and medal upon completing the race. After the run, enjoy refreshments, music, and a vibrant award ceremony.
